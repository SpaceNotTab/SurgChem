Quality Without Compromise

Customer-Centric Approach

At the heart of Surgchem's ethos lies a deep-rooted commitment to customer satisfaction. We take pride in building enduring relationships with our clients, both locally in Kerala and across the country. Our relentless pursuit of customer delight drives us to continually expand our product line and services to meet the dynamic needs of our esteemed clientele

Innovative Solutions

In addition to our core offerings, Surgchem specializes in bespoke solutions tailored to the unique requirements of our clients. Our in-house manufacturing capabilities allow us to produce surgical instruments customized to precise specifications, while our expertise in medical gas pipeline works ensures the safe and efficient delivery of essential gases within healthcare facilities.

Complementary Divisions

Surgchem's calibration division, Biomedical Techniques, and manufacturing division, BMT Technologies, complement our core offerings seamlessly. Biomedical Techniques specializes in the calibration of medical devices and quality assurance testing of diagnostic radiology equipment, accredited by NABL and authorized by the Atomic Energy Regulatory Board. We are also accredited by NSF United States of America for biosafety validation. On the other hand, BMT Technologies designs, manufactures, and distributes hospital furniture crafted to the highest standards of safety, functionality, and comfort.

Global Presence

Surgchem Group extends its reach beyond India, with a presence in the Sultanate of Oman for calibration and sales of medical equipment, ensuring that our commitment to excellence transcends geographical boundaries.

Our Commitment

At Surgchem and its associated entities, our mission is simple yet profound: to continue delivering excellence in healthcare equipment, services, and furniture. We remain steadfast in our dedication to empowering medical professionals with the tools they need to provide exceptional care to patients, ensuring a healthier and brighter future for all. With a strong workforce of 50 individuals spanning sales, manufacturing, and services, and robust logistics ensuring timely delivery, Surgchem is poised to lead the way in healthcare solutions for years to come.

Who We Are

We are one of the finest Surgical Equipment Dealers in Thrissur, Kerala ever since 1980. With over 40 years of experience as a major distributor of numerous branded medical devices, Surgchem has been supplying surgical instruments, hospital furniture, and biomedical devices to the medical field. Working closely with prominent hospitals in Kerala and beyond, we gained an understanding of how the quality of medical equipment plays a crucial role in patient safety.

We offer a wide range of products including surgical equipment, hospital equipment, dressing materials, specialized surgical equipment, veterinary instruments, wheelchairs, folding wheelchairs, disposable surgical products, nebulizers, hospital beds, etc.

We provide precision-made equipment at pocket-friendly prices to help hospitals provide quality services to the patients.

Our establishment acts as a one-stop destination servicing customers both local and from other parts of Kerala. Over the course of our journey, our business has established a firm foothold in its industry. The belief that customer satisfaction is as important as products and services has helped our establishment garner a vast base of customers, which continues to grow by the day. This business employs individuals who are dedicated towards their respective roles and put in a lot of effort to achieve the common vision and larger goals of the company. In the near future, we aim to expand our line of products and services and cater to a larger client base.
